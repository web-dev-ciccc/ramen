Established in 1978, Martinelli & Bambini Co. is one of the most traditional ramen restaurant in Vancouver. Founded by Lorenzo Martinelli in a small store in Hastings at Nanaimo just one year later that he has arrived in Canada. He brought all his family`s knowledge in culinary from Italy. His mother was from China and his father from Italy. Therefore, he mixed the Japanese and Italian culinary and created a new concept of ramen noodles. Lorenzo got married with a Canadian girl and they had two boys, James and Enrico. Nowadays, they help Lorenzo in the restaurant. In 2005, he moved the restaurant to Downtown. Now, the restaurant is bigger and is able to receive more people. The location and the people flow provided a big increase on the sales and made the restaurant being recognized not just in Vancouver, but also in neighboring cities. In 2021 they are planning to open a branch in Gastown. His older son, James, is going to be the general manager.
